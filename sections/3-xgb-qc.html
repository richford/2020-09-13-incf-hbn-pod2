Community science QC

Challenge

  • We have the expert-rated subset. Now what?
  • The size of the HBN makes expert QC prohibitve for the full dataset.
  • We would like to obtain QC scores for future releases.
One of our expert raters (via GIPHY)

Solution

Community scientists amplify expert QC ratings

  • We created a web-app that presents views of b=0 and directionally-encoded FA maps for a pass/fail decision for 1,653 HBN subjects.
  • 374 community scientists provided 587,778 ratings for a mean of > 200 ratings per subject.
  • 133 new co-authors!
  • More generally, this is a two-tiered rating protocol:
    • Low volume, high burden tier (experts)
    • High volume, low burden tier (community)

Amplifying expert scores from Fibr raters

  • We trained a gradient boosting model to predict expert scores based on both Fibr ratings and automated QSIPrep metrics.
  • This outperformed a model trained on only the QSIPrep metrics, demonstrating the added value of the community science raters.
